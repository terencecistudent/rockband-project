History about The Monkees:

  • September 8th 1965

    An ad appears on 'Variety' looking for "Four Insane Boys" to star in a TV show.

  • July 19th 1966

    The Monkees records theme song to their TV show.

  • August 16th 1966

    Release their first single : Last Train To Clarksville.

  • September 12th 1966

    The Monkees TV Show makes its debut.

  • June 4th 1967

    The Monkees win an Emmy Award for Outstanding Comedy Series.

  • December 2nd 1967

    The Monkees 'Pisces, Aquarius, Capricorn and Jones album hits' No.1.

  • August 19th 1968

    Final episode of TV Show The Monkees airs.

  • November 6th 1968

    The Monkees film 'Head' is released.

  • August 11th 1986

    The Monkees on a reunion tour, have six albums on the Billboard 200.

  • February 29th 2012

    Davy Jones dies of a heartattack at 66 years old.
